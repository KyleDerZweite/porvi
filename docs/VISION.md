# Porvi Vision

## What Porvi Is Trying To Preserve

Porvi exists to preserve the feeling of an individualized creator website.

The point is not to produce interchangeable profile pages. The point is to keep
room for sites that feel authored, specific, and visibly different from generic
creator tools.

## Why Contract-First Matters

A stable contract is what makes the platform layer possible without flattening
everything into sameness.

The contract gives Porvi a durable surface for:

- validation
- editing
- preview and publish workflows
- import and export
- supervised AI changes

Without that layer, every improvement becomes a one-off site exercise again.

## Why Static Output Still Matters

Static Astro output keeps the published site portable, cheap to host, and easy
to reason about.

Porvi can add a meaningful platform around the site without forcing the
public-facing artifact itself to become a dynamic application.

## Why Individualized Creator Sites Still Matter

Porvi should not optimize toward a commodity link-in-bio result.

The product direction is a stable contract base with room for deeply
individualized implementation above it. A creator should still be able to end up
with a site that feels hand-crafted, even when the platform manages the
surrounding workflow.

## What Porvi Is Not

- a generic drag-and-drop site builder
- a commodity link-in-bio clone
- a first-party password product
- a platform that treats creator sites as interchangeable templates
- a system where the platform layer replaces the need for intentional site craft
